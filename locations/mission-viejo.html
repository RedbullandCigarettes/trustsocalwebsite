About Mission Viejo, California

Mission Viejo (population 95,638) is one of the largest master-planned communities in the United States. Known for its excellent schools, safe neighborhoods, and family-oriented atmosphere, it consistently ranks among America's safest cities.

The community's strong emphasis on appearance and achievement can make it difficult for families to admit when they're struggling. Many parents hide addiction, fearful of judgment from neighbors, church communities, and their children's schools.

Trust SoCal provides the confidential, family-centered treatment that Mission Viejo families need. We understand the pressure to maintain appearances, and we offer a judgment-free environment where healing can begin.
