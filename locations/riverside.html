Serving the Riverside Community

Riverside is the largest city in California's Inland Empire with a population exceeding 315,000. As the county seat of Riverside County, it serves as the economic and cultural hub for the entire region. Unfortunately, the Inland Empire has been significantly impacted by the opioid epidemic, with Riverside County experiencing over 900 overdose deaths in 2023. Fentanyl-related fatalities have increased dramatically, affecting families across all demographics from UC Riverside students to longtime residents of historic neighborhoods.

Trust SoCal proudly serves Riverside residents seeking quality addiction treatment. Our Orange County facility is approximately 35-40 minutes from downtown Riverside via the 91 Freeway. We specialize in treating alcohol addiction, opioid dependency, methamphetamine addiction, and co-occurring mental health disorders. Our programs include medical detox, residential treatment, and flexible outpatient programs.

Directions from Riverside

From Downtown Riverside: Take the 91 Freeway West through Corona and into Orange County. The drive takes approximately 35-45 minutes depending on traffic. From La Sierra or western Riverside, you may also take the 15 Freeway South to the 91 West.

Need transportation? Call (555) 123-4567 and we'll arrange complimentary pickup from anywhere in Riverside, including the Metrolink Downtown Riverside station or UC Riverside campus.

Why Riverside Residents Choose Treatment in Orange County

Distance Creates Focus

Being 35-40 minutes from home removes you from triggers, dealers, and enabling relationships. Many Riverside residents find that the separation helps them focus entirely on recovery without distractions from their daily environment.

Fresh Start Environment

Our Orange County facility offers a peaceful, therapeutic environment away from the stress and familiarity of home. The change of scenery helps many clients approach recovery with a fresh perspective and renewed commitment.

Close Enough for Family

While offering healthy distance, we're still close enough for family visits during designated times. Our family program includes weekly sessions where loved ones can participate in healing without the client being too far from home.

Frequently Asked Questions: Drug Rehab for Riverside Residents

How far is Trust SoCal from Riverside?

Our treatment facility in Orange County is approximately 35-45 minutes from downtown Riverside via the 91 Freeway West. We serve all of Riverside County including Corona, Moreno Valley, Murrieta, and Temecula.

Do you offer transportation from Riverside?

Yes, we provide complimentary transportation for all Riverside County residents entering treatment. We can pick you up from home, the Downtown Riverside Metrolink station, or any location in the Inland Empire. Call (555) 123-4567 to arrange pickup.

Do you accept IEHP (Inland Empire Health Plan)?

We work with most insurance plans including those common in Riverside County. While IEHP coverage varies, we also accept Medi-Cal, Anthem, Cigna, Aetna, United Healthcare, and many others. Contact us for a free insurance verification to confirm your benefits.

Why should I go to Orange County instead of staying in Riverside?

Many addiction specialists recommend treatment away from home to remove triggers, dealers, and enabling relationships. Our Orange County location is close enough for family visits but far enough to help you focus entirely on recovery without distractions from your daily environment.

Can my family visit during treatment?

Absolutely. We have designated family visitation times and encourage participation in our family program. Riverside is close enough that family members can attend weekly family therapy sessions and visit during approved times without extensive travel.
