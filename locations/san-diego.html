Addiction Treatment for San Diego

San Diego—California's second-largest city—faces significant addiction challenges. The border region sees high availability of drugs including fentanyl and methamphetamine, while the military presence, university population, and tourist economy each bring their own substance use patterns.

Trust SoCal serves San Diego residents from our Orange County facility, providing quality treatment with enough distance to break old patterns while remaining accessible for family connections and aftercare planning.

San Diego Area Addiction Challenges

  • Border Proximity: Easy access to drugs trafficked from Mexico
  • Fentanyl Crisis: High rates of fentanyl-related overdoses
  • Methamphetamine: Significant meth use and distribution
  • Military Population: Veterans and active duty with unique needs
  • College Students: SDSU, UCSD, and other universities

Getting Here from San Diego

Transportation

  • By Car: 60-90 minutes via I-5 North (depending on traffic and starting point)
  • Our Transport: We provide transportation from San Diego for admissions
  • Train: Pacific Surfliner connects San Diego to OC stations

We coordinate transportation for all San Diego admissions. Call us to arrange pickup from anywhere in San Diego County.

Why Travel North for Treatment?

  • Distance from triggers: Away from San Diego's using environment and connections
  • Change of scenery: New environment supports new behaviors
  • Focus on recovery: Remove distractions of daily life
  • Family accessible: Still close enough for family involvement
  • Quality accredited care: Joint Commission accredited facility

For Military Personnel & Veterans

San Diego is home to major military installations including Naval Base San Diego, Marine Corps Base Camp Pendleton, and others. We understand military culture and provide:

  • Veteran-specific programming
  • TRICARE accepted
  • Understanding of combat trauma and PTSD
  • Military sexual trauma support
  • Transition assistance

Treatment Programs

  • Medical Detox - Safe withdrawal management
  • Residential Treatment - 30-90 day immersive programs
  • Partial Hospitalization - Full-day programming
  • Intensive Outpatient - Flexible scheduling

Returning to San Diego

We help you plan for successful return to San Diego including connecting with local therapists, psychiatrists, support groups, and sober living options. Your aftercare plan begins during treatment so you have support waiting when you return home.
