Serving the Huntington Beach Community

Huntington Beach - "Surf City USA" - faces unique addiction challenges tied to its beach culture. With a population of over 198,000, Huntington Beach is Orange County's fourth-largest city. The laid-back surf culture can mask serious substance abuse issues, from alcohol at beach bonfires to prescription drug misuse among aging surfers dealing with injuries. Orange County recorded over 700 overdose deaths in 2023, and coastal communities are not immune.

Trust SoCal understands the Huntington Beach lifestyle. Whether you're struggling with alcohol addiction that started as social drinking, opioid dependency from a surfing injury, or other substance use challenges, we provide comprehensive care including medical detox, residential treatment, and flexible outpatient programs.

Frequently Asked Questions: Huntington Beach Drug Rehab

How far is Trust SoCal from Huntington Beach?

Our treatment facility is located approximately 15-25 minutes from downtown Huntington Beach via the 405 or 55 freeways. We're easily accessible from all HB neighborhoods including downtown Main Street, Huntington Harbour, and Seacliff areas.

Do you offer transportation from Huntington Beach?

Yes, we provide complimentary transportation for Huntington Beach residents. We can pick you up from your home, the beach area, or any location in HB. Call (555) 123-4567 to arrange pickup.

What insurance plans do you accept for Huntington Beach residents?

We accept most major insurance plans including Aetna, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Cigna, Kaiser, United Healthcare, and Medi-Cal. Many Huntington Beach residents have coverage through Boeing, oil industry employers, and local businesses. Visit our insurance page for free verification.

Are there AA/NA meetings in Huntington Beach?

Yes, Huntington Beach has an active recovery community with multiple AA and NA meetings daily, including beach-area meetings. The OC AA Central Office at (714) 556-4555 can provide current schedules. We help connect patients with local support groups as part of aftercare planning.

Do you treat alcohol addiction from beach culture drinking?

Absolutely. We understand how the HB beach lifestyle can normalize heavy drinking - from bonfires to bar hopping on Main Street. Our alcohol addiction treatment program addresses these patterns with evidence-based therapy and helps you build a sober lifestyle you can enjoy.
