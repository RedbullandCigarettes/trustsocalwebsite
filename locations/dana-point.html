About Dana Point, California

Dana Point (population 33,107) is a coastal gem named after author Richard Henry Dana Jr. The city is home to Dana Point Harbor, world-class surfing at Doheny State Beach, and one of California's most picturesque headlands.

The harbor social scene—yacht clubs, waterfront restaurants, sunset cocktails—creates an environment where drinking is part of daily life. For some residents, what starts as social drinking quietly becomes dependency.

Trust SoCal offers Dana Point residents confidential treatment away from the harbor scene. We understand the social pressures and provide a fresh environment for genuine recovery.

What insurance plans do you accept for Dana Point residents?
