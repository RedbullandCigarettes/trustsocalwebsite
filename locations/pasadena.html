Treatment Programs for Pasadena Residents

Comprehensive addiction care from medical detox through aftercare and alumni support.

Medical Detox

Safe, comfortable withdrawal management with 24/7 medical supervision. Our board-certified physicians use FDA-approved medications to minimize discomfort and ensure your safety during this critical first step.

Duration: 3-7 days

Residential Treatment

Intensive inpatient care combining individual therapy, group counseling, family involvement, and holistic healing. Our structured environment supports deep personal work and lasting change.

Duration: 30-90 days

Partial Hospitalization (PHP)

Day treatment providing intensive programming while allowing more flexibility. Ideal for those stepping down from residential care or those with stable local housing.

Schedule: 5-6 hours/day, 5-7 days/week

Intensive Outpatient (IOP)

Flexible treatment for those who need to maintain work or family responsibilities. Evening and weekend options available for Pasadena professionals.

Schedule: 3-4 hours/day, 3-5 days/week

About Pasadena, California

Understanding the community we serve.

Pasadena is known worldwide for the Rose Bowl, the Tournament of Roses, and prestigious institutions like the California Institute of Technology (Caltech) and NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL). This city of approximately 138,000 residents combines historic charm with cutting-edge innovation.

The pressure of working at elite institutions, the stress of academic competition, and the social culture of Old Pasadena can all contribute to substance abuse issues. We've helped researchers, engineers, artists, healthcare workers, and families from throughout the Pasadena area find lasting recovery.
