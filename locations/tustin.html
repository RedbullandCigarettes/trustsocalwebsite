Tustin is a city with deep military roots—the former Tustin Marine Corps Air Station remains part of its identity. Many residents have military connections, and the community is home to veterans, military families, and defense industry professionals.

Understanding Military-Connected Addiction

  • PTSD and trauma often lead to self-medication with alcohol or drugs
  • Prescription pain medication dependency from service-related injuries
  • Transition challenges for veterans adjusting to civilian life
  • Military culture that can stigmatize seeking mental health treatment

Military-Informed Treatment

  • Staff experienced with military culture and veteran needs
  • TRICARE accepted and VA coordination available
  • Trauma-informed care addressing underlying PTSD and MST
  • Peer support from other veterans in recovery

Serving the Tustin Community

Orange County faces a significant addiction crisis, with over 500 overdose deaths reported annually and fentanyl now present in the majority of fatal overdoses. Tustin, with its population of approximately 80,000 residents, is not immune to these challenges. The city's military heritage means many residents and families are dealing with service-related trauma that can contribute to substance abuse.

About Tustin, California

Tustin (population 80,276) is centrally located in Orange County with a rich military heritage from the former Marine Corps Air Station. The city's historic downtown charm and suburban neighborhoods make it popular with families and professionals.

Many Tustin residents have connections to military service, whether through personal service, family members, or careers in the defense industry. This creates a community where both the unique challenges and stigmas around addiction and mental health are particularly relevant.

Trust SoCal honors military service and understands military culture. We provide confidential, effective treatment for veterans and their families, with staff who respect your service and know how to help.

Frequently Asked Questions: Tustin Rehab

Do you accept TRICARE?

Yes, we accept TRICARE and work with the VA when appropriate. Call our admissions team for details on coverage and benefits verification.

Do you treat PTSD alongside addiction?

Absolutely. Our dual diagnosis program provides integrated treatment for both conditions. We use trauma-informed approaches including EMDR and CPT for veterans.

Will treatment affect my security clearance?

Seeking treatment is protected under federal regulations. In most cases, voluntarily seeking help demonstrates responsibility and can actually protect your career and clearance.

Are there other veterans in treatment?

Yes. Many of our patients are veterans or have military connections. The peer support from others who understand military culture is often cited as a key part of recovery.
