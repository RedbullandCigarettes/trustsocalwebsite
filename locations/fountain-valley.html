Serving the Fountain Valley Community

Fountain Valley (population approximately 57,000) is a family-oriented community in central Orange County known for its parks and quality schools. However, like all of Orange County, Fountain Valley is affected by the regional addiction crisis. Orange County sees over 500 overdose deaths annually, with fentanyl present in the majority of fatal overdoses. Many Fountain Valley families are dealing with addiction privately, unsure where to turn for help.

Frequently Asked Questions: Fountain Valley Rehab

How far is Trust SoCal from Fountain Valley?

Trust SoCal is located approximately 15-20 minutes from Fountain Valley. We are easily accessible via the 405 freeway and serve all Fountain Valley neighborhoods.

Do you offer transportation from Fountain Valley?

Yes, we provide complimentary transportation coordination for Fountain Valley residents. We can arrange pickup from your home or any location in the Fountain Valley area.

What insurance plans do you accept for Fountain Valley residents?

We accept most major insurance plans including Anthem Blue Cross, Aetna, Cigna, United Healthcare, and many others. Verify your insurance for free.

Are there local support meetings near Fountain Valley?

Yes, there are numerous AA and NA meetings in Fountain Valley and surrounding areas including Huntington Beach and Costa Mesa. The Orange County Central Office (714-556-4555) can help locate meetings.

Does Fountain Valley Regional Hospital handle overdoses?

Yes, Fountain Valley Regional Hospital has an emergency department for immediate crisis care. However, for comprehensive addiction treatment and long-term recovery, a specialized treatment center provides the ongoing care needed for lasting sobriety.
