About Newport Beach, California

Newport Beach (population 85,239) is one of California's most affluent coastal communities. Home to Newport Harbor, Fashion Island, and the Balboa Peninsula, it attracts successful entrepreneurs, executives, and retirees seeking the California dream.

The social scene in Newport Beach often revolves around events where alcohol flows freely—yacht club gatherings, country club dinners, charity galas, and beach parties. This environment can make it difficult to recognize when social drinking crosses into dependency.
