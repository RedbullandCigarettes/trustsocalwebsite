High-Functioning Addiction

Many Santa Monica professionals maintain careers and outward success while struggling with addiction. This "high-functioning" pattern often delays treatment as people convince themselves the problem isn't serious. Signs include:

  • Needing substances to manage social situations
  • Drinking more than intended at networking events
  • Using prescription medications beyond their intended purpose
  • Morning anxiety relieved only by substances
  • Consequences mounting despite professional success

Common Substances in Santa Monica

  • Alcohol: Normalized in upscale social environments
  • Cocaine: Associated with entertainment and high-pressure careers
  • Prescription medications: Easy access, perceived as "safer"
  • Benzodiazepines: For anxiety, often prescribed by multiple doctors
  • Cannabis: Accepted culture, but can become problematic
