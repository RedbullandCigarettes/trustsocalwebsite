About Costa Mesa, California

Costa Mesa (population 112,174) is known as the "City of the Arts" and serves as a major retail and cultural destination in Orange County. Home to South Coast Plaza—one of the largest shopping centers in the U.S.—Costa Mesa attracts visitors and workers from throughout Southern California.

The city's extensive restaurant and nightlife scene along 17th Street and in the Triangle Square area creates an environment where drinking is deeply normalized. For hospitality workers especially, the line between work culture and addiction can blur.

Trust SoCal understands the unique challenges facing Costa Mesa residents. Our treatment center provides a supportive environment away from local triggers while remaining close enough for family involvement in your recovery.

Frequently Asked Questions: Costa Mesa Rehab

Is there a drug rehab near Costa Mesa?

Trust SoCal's Joint Commission-accredited facility is located nearby in Orange County, just 10-20 minutes from Costa Mesa. We serve Costa Mesa residents with transportation assistance included. Learn more about our residential treatment and medical detox programs.

I work in hospitality - can I get treatment?

Absolutely. Many of our patients work in Costa Mesa's restaurant, bar, and retail industries. We offer flexible outpatient programs and understand the unique challenges facing hospitality workers, including schedule flexibility needs.

What insurance plans do you accept for Costa Mesa residents?

We accept most major insurance plans including Aetna, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Cigna, Kaiser, United Healthcare, and those offered by South Coast Plaza employers, Segerstrom Center, and other local businesses. Visit our insurance page for free verification.

Are there AA/NA meetings in Costa Mesa?

Yes, Costa Mesa has active AA and NA communities. The Costa Mesa Alano Club at 1900 Newport Blvd hosts daily meetings. The OC AA Central Office at (714) 556-4555 can help you find meetings. We also connect patients with local support groups during aftercare planning.

Do you offer transportation from Costa Mesa?

Yes, we provide free transportation for Costa Mesa residents. We can pick you up from home, work, John Wayne Airport (SNA), or any location in Costa Mesa. Just call (555) 123-4567 to arrange.
