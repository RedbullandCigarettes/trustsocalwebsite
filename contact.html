What to Expect

The Admissions Process

Step 1: Initial Contact

Call us or complete the contact form. You'll speak with a compassionate admissions specialist who will listen to your situation without judgment and answer all your questions about treatment.

Step 2: Free Assessment

We'll conduct a confidential assessment to understand your needs, addiction history, medical conditions, and treatment goals. This helps us recommend the right level of care for your situation.

Step 3: Insurance Verification

Our team will verify your insurance benefits and provide a clear breakdown of coverage, costs, and payment options. We handle all communication with your insurance company.

Step 4: Admission

Once you're ready, we can arrange same-day admission. We'll coordinate transportation if needed, help you prepare for treatment, and answer any final questions before you arrive.

Step 5: Welcome to Treatment

Upon arrival, our team will welcome you, complete intake paperwork, conduct a medical evaluation, and get you settled into your room. Your treatment journey begins immediately.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer same-day admission?

Yes! We understand that addiction is a crisis that requires immediate help. We offer same-day admission when clinically appropriate and can arrange transportation if needed.

Is my information confidential?

Absolutely. All communication and treatment is protected by strict confidentiality laws including HIPAA. We will never share your information without your written consent.

How long does treatment take?

Treatment length varies based on individual needs. Detox typically takes 3-7 days, residential treatment ranges from 30-90 days, and outpatient programs last 12+ weeks. We'll recommend the appropriate length based on your assessment.

Can I visit before enrolling?

Yes! We encourage prospective clients and families to tour our facility. Tours are available Monday-Sunday from 9am-7pm by appointment. Call (555) 123-4567 to schedule.

What should I bring to treatment?

Bring comfortable clothing, personal hygiene items, any necessary medications (in original containers), insurance information, and photo ID. We'll provide a detailed packing list when you're admitted. Don't let not having everything ready delay getting help‚Äîwe can provide essentials if needed.

Will my insurance cover treatment?

Most major insurance plans cover addiction treatment. We'll verify your specific benefits at no cost and explain your coverage before admission. We also offer payment plans and financial assistance for those who need it.
