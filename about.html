Our Story

A Legacy of Healing and Hope

Trust SoCal was founded on a simple but powerful belief: that every person struggling with addiction deserves access to world-class treatment delivered with compassion, dignity, and respect.

Our journey began over 15 years ago when a small group of addiction medicine specialists, licensed therapists, and recovery advocates came together with a shared vision: to create a treatment center that would set a new standard for excellence in addiction care. They had witnessed firsthand how traditional treatment programs often fell short‚Äîfailing to address the whole person, rushing clients through cookie-cutter programs, or treating addiction as a moral failing rather than the complex medical condition it truly is.

From our very first client, we've been committed to doing things differently. We invested in building a comprehensive continuum of care that addresses every stage of recovery, from medically supervised detox to long-term aftercare. We recruited the most experienced, compassionate professionals in the field‚Äîboard-certified addiction medicine physicians, licensed clinical psychologists, credentialed therapists, and certified recovery coaches who bring both clinical expertise and genuine empathy to their work.

Today, Trust SoCal stands as Orange County's premier addiction treatment center, recognized for our clinical excellence, innovative treatment approaches, and exceptional outcomes. We've helped thousands of individuals break free from the chains of addiction and rebuild meaningful, fulfilling lives. Our 85% success rate‚Äîmeasured by clients maintaining sobriety one year post-treatment‚Äîis a testament to the effectiveness of our comprehensive, personalized approach.

What truly sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to treating the whole person. We understand that addiction doesn't exist in isolation‚Äîit's intertwined with trauma, mental health conditions, relationship challenges, and underlying pain that often goes unaddressed in traditional treatment programs. That's why our approach integrates evidence-based medical treatment with comprehensive mental health care, family healing, and holistic wellness therapies that address mind, body, and spirit.

Our Joint Commission accreditation is more than just a badge on our website‚Äîit represents our commitment to maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and ethical practice in addiction treatment. Joint Commission (Commission on Accreditation of Rehabilitation Facilities) is the gold standard in behavioral health accreditation, and earning this designation requires demonstrating excellence in every aspect of our operations, from clinical protocols to facility safety to client satisfaction. We undergo rigorous evaluation every three years to maintain this accreditation, ensuring we continuously improve and evolve our services.

Through the years, we've remained true to our founding principles while continuously evolving to incorporate the latest advances in addiction science, therapeutic techniques, and wellness practices. We've expanded our programs, enhanced our facilities, and deepened our expertise‚Äîbut our core mission remains unchanged: to provide the highest quality addiction treatment in an environment of compassion, respect, and hope.

Meet Our Team

Expert, Compassionate Professionals

Our multidisciplinary team brings together the finest professionals in addiction medicine, mental health, and holistic wellness to provide comprehensive, integrated care.

At Trust SoCal, you'll be cared for by a team of highly trained, deeply compassionate professionals who are experts in their respective fields. Our multidisciplinary approach means you benefit from diverse perspectives and specialized expertise, all working together to support your recovery.

Medical Director & Physicians

Our board-certified addiction medicine physicians oversee all medical aspects of treatment, from detox management to medication-assisted treatment to ongoing medical care. With decades of combined experience, they provide expert clinical leadership and ensure the highest standards of medical care.

Clinical Director & Therapists

Our licensed clinical psychologists and therapists (LCSWs, LMFTs, LPCCs) provide individual therapy, group counseling, and family sessions. Each therapist brings specialized training in evidence-based modalities including CBT, DBT, trauma-informed care, and motivational interviewing.

Nursing Staff

Our registered nurses (RNs) and licensed vocational nurses (LVNs) provide 24/7 medical monitoring, medication administration, and compassionate support throughout your treatment. Many have specialized certifications in addiction nursing (CARN) and bring years of experience in addiction medicine.

Recovery Coaches & Case Managers

Our certified recovery coaches and case managers provide daily support, help you navigate treatment, coordinate care, and assist with discharge planning. Many are in recovery themselves, bringing lived experience and authentic understanding to their work.

Holistic Wellness Team

Our wellness coordinators, yoga instructors, art therapists, nutritionists, and fitness trainers provide complementary therapies that support whole-person healing. These specialized professionals help you develop healthy coping skills and sustainable wellness practices.

Every member of our team is committed to your success. We invest heavily in ongoing professional development, ensuring our staff stays current with the latest research and best practices in addiction treatment. More importantly, we hire for compassion and empathy‚Äîqualities that can't be taught but make all the difference in creating a healing environment.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Trust SoCal

Get answers to the most common questions about our addiction treatment center in Orange County.

What makes Trust SoCal different from other rehab centers?

Trust SoCal stands out through our Joint Commission accreditation (the gold standard in treatment quality), our 85% success rate (significantly above industry average), our 15+ years of experience, and our comprehensive continuum of care from medical detox through aftercare. We also specialize in dual diagnosis treatment, addressing both addiction and co-occurring mental health conditions. Our luxury facilities and low client-to-staff ratios ensure personalized, high-quality care.

What types of addiction does Trust SoCal treat?

We treat all types of substance use disorders including alcohol addiction, opioid addiction, heroin addiction, fentanyl addiction, cocaine addiction, methamphetamine addiction, benzodiazepine addiction, and prescription drug addiction. We also treat co-occurring mental health disorders like depression, anxiety, PTSD, and bipolar disorder.

Is Trust SoCal accredited?

Yes. Trust SoCal is accredited by Joint Commission (Commission on Accreditation of Rehabilitation Facilities), which is the gold standard for behavioral health treatment. Joint Commission accreditation requires rigorous external evaluation of our programs, staff qualifications, safety protocols, and outcomes. We are also licensed by California's Department of Health Care Services (DHCS). These credentials ensure you receive care that meets the highest quality and safety standards.

Where is Trust SoCal located?

Trust SoCal is located in Orange County, California. We serve clients from throughout Southern California including Santa Ana, Irvine, Anaheim, Costa Mesa, Newport Beach, Huntington Beach, Los Angeles, San Diego, and beyond. Our location offers year-round mild weather, beautiful surroundings, and a strong recovery community‚Äîideal conditions for healing.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans including Aetna, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Cigna, United Healthcare, and many others. Our admissions team provides free, confidential insurance verification to help you understand your coverage before you commit to treatment. We also offer private pay options and can discuss financing if needed.

Can families visit during treatment?

Yes. We believe family involvement is crucial to successful recovery. Our Family Program includes family therapy sessions, family education workshops, and designated visiting times. We also offer family weekend programs where loved ones can participate in treatment alongside the client. Family members can also access Al-Anon and other support resources to support their own healing.
