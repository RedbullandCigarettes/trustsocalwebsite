Family Addiction Recovery Program in Orange County

Healing families affected by addiction through comprehensive therapy, education, and support services. Rebuild trust, improve communication, and create lasting recovery together.

Get Started Today Contact us to begin your family's recovery journey

Comprehensive Family Support for Addiction Recovery

Addiction affects the entire family system. Our evidence-based family program addresses the complex dynamics that develop when addiction is present, helping families heal together and build stronger, healthier relationships.

Family Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation of family dynamics, communication patterns, and the impact of addiction on relationships.

Group Family Therapy

Structured group sessions with other families in recovery, providing peer support and shared learning experiences.

Individual Family Counseling

One-on-one counseling sessions tailored to address specific family needs and challenges.

Educational Workshops

Learn about addiction, codependency, healthy boundaries, and effective communication strategies.

Benefits of Our Family Program

  • Improved family communication and conflict resolution skills
  • Understanding of addiction as a disease affecting the whole family
  • Development of healthy boundaries and coping strategies
  • Reduced enabling behaviors and codependency patterns
  • Stronger family support system for long-term recovery
  • Tools for rebuilding trust and repairing relationships
