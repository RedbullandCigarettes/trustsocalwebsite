Comprehensive Addiction Treatment Services in Orange County

Trust SoCal provides evidence-based addiction treatment programs designed to help you or your loved one achieve lasting recovery. Our compassionate team offers personalized care in a supportive environment.

Our Treatment Programs

We offer a full continuum of care to meet you wherever you are in your recovery journey.

Medical Detox

Our medically supervised detox program provides safe, comfortable withdrawal management with 24/7 medical monitoring. Our experienced medical team ensures your safety and comfort during this critical first step.

  • 24/7 medical supervision
  • Medication-assisted treatment
  • Comfortable, private rooms
  • Nutritional support
Learn More About Medical Detox

Residential Treatment

Our 30-90 day residential program provides intensive, round-the-clock care in a structured, supportive environment. Focus entirely on your recovery with comprehensive therapeutic services.

  • Individual and group therapy
  • Family therapy sessions
  • Recreational therapy
  • Life skills training
Learn More About Residential Treatment

Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP)

Our PHP offers intensive day treatment while allowing you to return home each evening. Perfect for those who need structured support but want to maintain some independence.

  • 6 hours of treatment daily
  • 5-7 days per week
  • Individual and group counseling
  • Psychiatric services
Learn More About PHP

Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP)

Our flexible IOP allows you to maintain work, school, or family commitments while receiving comprehensive addiction treatment. Evening and weekend options available.

  • 3 hours per session
  • 3-4 days per week
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Ongoing support groups
Learn More About IOP

Frequently Asked Questions

What addiction treatment services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive addiction treatment services including medical detox, residential treatment, partial hospitalization programs, and intensive outpatient programs. All programs are medically supervised with 24/7 staff support.

Do you accept insurance for addiction treatment?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Our admissions team can verify your benefits and help determine your coverage for addiction treatment services.

Is help available 24/7?

Yes, we provide 24/7 crisis support and our admission specialists are available around the clock to help you or your loved one get the treatment needed.
