What is Aftercare?

Aftercare is ongoing support after completing primary treatment. Recovery is a lifelong journey, and aftercare ensures you have the tools, resources, and community needed to maintain sobriety for the long haul. Whether you finished residential treatment, PHP, or IOP, aftercare keeps you connected and accountable.

At Trust SoCal, we believe recovery doesn't have an expiration date. Our aftercare services are available to all alumni‚Äîwhether you completed treatment last month or ten years ago. We're here for you.

Who Benefits from Aftercare?

Aftercare is for everyone in recovery, especially:

  • Anyone who has completed treatment and wants continued support
  • Individuals transitioning back to independent living after sober living
  • People facing new life stressors or triggers
  • Those who want to stay connected to the recovery community
  • Alumni looking to give back and mentor others in early recovery
