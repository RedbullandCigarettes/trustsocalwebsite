Does Blue Cross Blue Shield Cover Drug and Alcohol Rehab?

Yes, Blue Cross Blue Shield provides coverage for drug and alcohol addiction treatment. As one of the largest health insurance providers in the United States, BCBS is required under the Mental Health Parity and Addiction Equity Act to cover substance abuse treatment at the same level as other medical conditions.

BCBS Addiction Treatment Coverage Includes:

  • Medical Detoxification: 24/7 supervised withdrawal management
  • Residential/Inpatient Treatment: 30-90 days depending on medical necessity
  • Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP): Day treatment programs
  • Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP): Flexible evening and weekend options
  • Outpatient Therapy: Individual and group counseling
  • Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT): Suboxone, Vivitrol, and other medications

Frequently Asked Questions About BCBS Rehab Coverage

Does Blue Cross Blue Shield cover drug rehab?

Yes, Blue Cross Blue Shield covers drug and alcohol rehab as part of their mental health and substance abuse benefits. Coverage typically includes medical detox, residential treatment, partial hospitalization (PHP), and intensive outpatient programs (IOP). Coverage levels vary by specific plan and state.

How much does BCBS pay for rehab?

Blue Cross Blue Shield typically covers 60-80% of in-network addiction treatment costs after the deductible. Coverage varies by plan type (PPO, HMO, EPO), deductible amount, and whether the provider is in-network. Trust SoCal offers free insurance verification to determine your exact coverage.
