TRICARE Addiction Treatment Benefits

TRICARE provides comprehensive coverage for substance use disorder treatment. If you're an active duty service member, retiree, or family member covered by TRICARE, you may have access to medical detox, residential treatment, outpatient programs, and ongoing aftercare support.

Trust SoCal is proud to work with TRICARE beneficiaries. We understand military culture, the unique stressors of service, and the challenges of seeking treatment while maintaining your career or supporting a military family.

TRICARE Plans We Accept

  • TRICARE Prime: Active duty and enrolled beneficiaries
  • TRICARE Select: Self-managed option for retirees and families
  • TRICARE Reserve Select: For National Guard and Reserve members
  • TRICARE Young Adult: For dependent adult children
  • TRICARE For Life: Medicare-eligible beneficiaries

Covered Treatment Services

TRICARE generally covers these addiction treatment services:

  • Medical Detoxification: Inpatient detox with 24/7 medical monitoring
  • Residential Treatment: Inpatient rehabilitation programs
  • Partial Hospitalization (PHP): Day treatment programs
  • Intensive Outpatient (IOP): Flexible treatment while living at home
  • Individual and Group Therapy: Evidence-based therapeutic interventions
  • Medication-Assisted Treatment: When clinically appropriate

Authorization Requirements

TRICARE typically requires prior authorization for addiction treatment services. Our admissions team handles the authorization process for you, including:

  • Contacting TRICARE to verify benefits
  • Submitting authorization requests
  • Providing clinical documentation
  • Following up on approvals
  • Handling appeals if needed

For Active Duty Service Members

Active duty members face unique considerations when seeking addiction treatment:

  • Treatment may require commander notification or approval
  • Some programs may affect security clearances (we can discuss confidentially)
  • Time away for treatment may be categorized as medical leave
  • TRICARE Prime requires referral from your primary care manager

Many active duty members delay treatment out of fear for their careers. In reality, seeking treatment often has better career outcomes than continued substance use. We can discuss these concerns confidentially.

For Military Families

Military family members covered by TRICARE have access to addiction treatment benefits. The stress of military life—deployments, relocations, worry about loved ones—can contribute to substance use problems. You deserve treatment that understands these unique pressures.

Our Veteran-Informed Care

Trust SoCal offers specialized programming for military-connected clients:

  • Veteran-specific groups with peers who understand military culture
  • Trauma treatment including EMDR for combat-related PTSD
  • Understanding of military sexual trauma (MST)
  • Support for transitioning service members
  • Moral injury work

Cost Sharing

Depending on your TRICARE plan, you may have:

  • TRICARE Prime: No cost share for active duty; minimal cost share for others
  • TRICARE Select: Annual deductible plus cost shares (typically 15-25%)
  • Catastrophic Cap: Annual out-of-pocket maximum protects you from excessive costs

We verify your specific benefits before treatment begins so you know what to expect.
