Does United Healthcare Cover Drug and Alcohol Rehab?

Yes, United Healthcare provides coverage for drug and alcohol addiction treatment. As the largest health insurer in the United States, UHC is required under the Mental Health Parity and Addiction Equity Act to cover substance abuse treatment. Behavioral health benefits are managed through Optum.

United Healthcare Addiction Treatment Coverage Includes:

  • Medical Detoxification: 24/7 supervised withdrawal management
  • Residential/Inpatient Treatment: Coverage based on medical necessity
  • Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP): Day treatment programs
  • Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP): Flexible evening/weekend options
  • Outpatient Therapy: Individual and group counseling
  • Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT): Suboxone, Vivitrol covered

Understanding Optum Behavioral Health

What Is Optum?

Optum Behavioral Health is UnitedHealth Group's behavioral health division. If you have United Healthcare insurance, your mental health and substance abuse benefits are managed by Optum. This includes:

  • Pre-authorization for addiction treatment
  • Utilization management and concurrent reviews
  • Network provider credentialing
  • Care coordination services

Trust SoCal works directly with Optum to handle all administrative requirements for your treatment.

Frequently Asked Questions About UHC Rehab Coverage

Does United Healthcare cover drug rehab?

Yes, United Healthcare covers drug and alcohol rehab through Optum Behavioral Health. Coverage includes medical detox, residential treatment, partial hospitalization (PHP), and intensive outpatient programs (IOP). UHC is one of the largest insurers in the US and is required to cover addiction treatment under federal parity laws.

How does Optum manage UHC behavioral health benefits?

Optum Behavioral Health manages substance abuse and mental health benefits for United Healthcare members. Optum handles pre-authorization, utilization reviews, and care coordination. Trust SoCal works directly with Optum to authorize treatment and handle all administrative requirements.

How much does United Healthcare pay for rehab?

United Healthcare typically covers 60-80% of in-network addiction treatment costs after the deductible. Coverage varies by plan type and whether services are in-network. Trust SoCal offers free insurance verification to determine your exact UHC benefits.

How do I use my UHC insurance for rehab?

Call Trust SoCal at (555) 123-4567 with your UHC member ID. We verify your benefits through Optum and obtain pre-authorization. Our admissions team handles all paperwork so you can focus on getting help.

What if Optum/UHC denies my treatment?

If Optum denies coverage, Trust SoCal's team files appeals using clinical documentation that supports medical necessity. We understand Optum's criteria and have success overturning denials. Don't let an initial denial prevent you from getting treatment.
