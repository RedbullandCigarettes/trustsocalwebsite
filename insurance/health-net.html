Does Health Net Cover Drug and Alcohol Rehab?

Yes, Health Net provides comprehensive coverage for addiction treatment. Under the Mental Health Parity and Addiction Equity Act, Health Net must cover substance abuse treatment at the same level as other medical conditions. As California's third-largest health plan, Health Net covers millions of Californians for behavioral health services.

Health Net Addiction Treatment Coverage Includes:

  • Medical Detoxification: 24/7 supervised withdrawal management
  • Residential/Inpatient Treatment: 30-90 days based on medical necessity
  • Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP): Day treatment programs
  • Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP): Flexible treatment options
  • Outpatient Therapy: Individual and group counseling
  • Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT): Suboxone, Vivitrol covered
