Does Aetna Cover Drug and Alcohol Rehab?

Yes, Aetna provides coverage for drug and alcohol addiction treatment. Under the Mental Health Parity and Addiction Equity Act, Aetna is required to cover substance abuse treatment at the same level as other medical conditions.

Aetna Addiction Treatment Coverage Includes:

  • Medical Detoxification: 24/7 supervised withdrawal management
  • Residential/Inpatient Treatment: 30-90 days depending on medical necessity
  • Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP): Day treatment programs
  • Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP): Flexible evening and weekend options
  • Outpatient Therapy: Individual and group counseling
  • Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT): Suboxone, Vivitrol, and other medications

Frequently Asked Questions About Aetna Rehab Coverage

Does Aetna cover drug rehab?

Yes, Aetna covers drug and alcohol rehab as part of their mental health and substance abuse benefits. Coverage includes medical detox, residential treatment, partial hospitalization (PHP), and intensive outpatient programs (IOP). Most Aetna plans cover 30-90 days of treatment depending on medical necessity.

How much does Aetna pay for rehab?

Aetna typically covers 60-80% of in-network addiction treatment costs after the deductible is met. Out-of-pocket costs vary based on your specific plan, deductible, copay, and out-of-pocket maximum. Trust SoCal offers free insurance verification to determine your exact coverage.

Is Trust SoCal in-network with Aetna?

Trust SoCal works with Aetna plans and can verify your benefits within minutes. We also work with out-of-network benefits and can help maximize your coverage. Contact our admissions team for immediate verification.

How do I use my Aetna insurance for rehab?

What if Aetna denies my claim?
