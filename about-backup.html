About Trust SoCal

Our Story

Trust SoCal was founded in 2015 with a mission to provide comprehensive, evidence-based addiction treatment in a compassionate environment. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to helping individuals and families overcome addiction and build lasting recovery.

Our Treatment Approach

We believe in treating the whole person, not just the addiction. Our comprehensive approach combines evidence-based therapies with holistic treatments to address the physical, emotional, and spiritual aspects of recovery.

Medical Care

24/7 medical supervision and medication-assisted treatment when appropriate.

Individual Therapy

One-on-one counseling sessions with licensed therapists specializing in addiction.

Group Therapy

Peer support groups and specialized therapy sessions for shared experiences.

Holistic Treatments

Yoga, meditation, art therapy, and other complementary healing modalities.
