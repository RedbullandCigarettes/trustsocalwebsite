Available Support Groups

Trust SoCal offers a comprehensive range of addiction support groups to meet diverse recovery needs. Our meetings provide safe, confidential environments for sharing experiences and building lasting connections.

Alcoholics Anonymous (AA)

12-step program meetings for alcohol addiction recovery. Open and closed meetings available throughout the week.

  • Daily meetings available
  • Beginner-friendly sessions
  • Step study groups
  • Speaker meetings

  • Literature study groups

  • Anniversary celebrations

Evidence-based addiction recovery support using cognitive-behavioral tools and techniques.
