Insurance We Accept

Working With Most Major Insurance Providers

Trust SoCal is in-network with most major insurance companies and we work diligently to maximize your benefits. We understand that navigating insurance coverage can be confusing and overwhelming‚Äîespecially during a crisis. That's why our experienced admissions team handles all the details for you.

The Mental Health Parity and Addiction Equity Act (MHPAEA) requires most insurance plans to cover addiction treatment at the same level as other medical conditions. This means your insurance likely covers a significant portion of treatment costs, including detox, residential care, outpatient programs, and therapy.

We've successfully worked with thousands of insurance plans to secure coverage for our clients. Our team has deep expertise in understanding policy details, maximizing benefits, and navigating pre-authorization requirements. We'll verify your coverage within hours and provide a clear breakdown of your expected costs before you begin treatment.

How It Works

Our Insurance Verification Process

Step 1: Free Benefits Verification

Call us at (555) 123-4567 or complete our online form. We'll verify your coverage within hours‚Äîoften while you're still on the phone. We need just your insurance card and basic information to get started.

Step 2: Coverage Breakdown

We'll provide a detailed explanation of your benefits including deductibles, co-pays, out-of-pocket maximums, and what services are covered. You'll know your estimated costs before beginning treatment.

Step 3: Pre-Authorization

Our team handles all pre-authorization requirements with your insurance company. We'll secure approval for treatment and ensure you have coverage for the appropriate level of care.

Step 4: Ongoing Management

Throughout treatment, we manage all insurance communications, submit claims, and handle any issues that arise. We'll also help with step-down authorizations as you transition through levels of care.

Payment Options

Out-of-Network Benefits & Payment Plans

Even if we're out-of-network with your insurance, you may still have substantial coverage. Many plans provide out-of-network benefits that reimburse 50-80% of treatment costs. We'll help you understand these benefits and submit claims for maximum reimbursement.

Flexible Payment Plans

We believe cost should never be a barrier to lifesaving treatment. That's why we offer flexible payment plans and financing options for both in-network and out-of-network clients.

  • No-interest payment plans available

  • Scholarships and financial assistance programs
