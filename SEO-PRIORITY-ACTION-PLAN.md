## CONTENT STRUCTURE TEMPLATE

### Every Priority Page Must Include:

1. **Hero Section**
   - H1 with primary keyword
   - Crisis statistics or urgency
   - Immediate CTA (phone + insurance verification)

2. **Introduction** (200-300 words)
   - Address search intent
   - Preview what page covers
   - Empathetic, professional tone

3. **Main Content** (3-7 sections, 500-800 words each)
   - H2: Understanding [Condition/Substance]
   - H2: Signs and Symptoms
   - H2: Treatment Approach
   - H2: Program Types (link to services)
   - H2: Timeline/What to Expect
   - H2: Why Trust SoCal

4. **FAQ Section** (800-1,200 words)
   - 10-15 questions
   - 60-80 words per answer
   - FAQ schema markup

5. **CTA Section**
   - Phone call
   - Insurance verification form
   - Free assessment link

6. **Internal Links** (10-15 per page)
   - Service pages (detox, residential, PHP, IOP)
   - Related condition pages
   - Insurance/admissions pages
   - Location pages
