Frequently Asked Questions

Does my insurance cover addiction treatment?

Most insurance plans are required to cover addiction treatment under the Mental Health Parity and Addiction Equity Act. We verify benefits with over 98% of insurance providers and help maximize your coverage.

How much will I pay out of pocket?

Most of our patients pay $0 out-of-pocket after insurance coverage. We provide free benefits verification to determine your exact coverage and any potential costs before treatment begins.

How quickly can you verify my insurance benefits?

We provide same-day insurance verification, typically within 2-4 hours. Our licensed benefits specialists are available 24/7 to help you understand your coverage and start treatment immediately.
